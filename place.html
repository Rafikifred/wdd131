About Rwanda

Rwanda, located in East Africa, is known as the "Land of a Thousand Hills". It is renowned for its breathtaking scenery, rich culture, and incredible biodiversity, including the endangered mountain gorillas.

Highlights

  • Kigali – Rwanda’s modern and vibrant capital city.
  • Volcanoes National Park – home of the mountain gorillas.
  • Lake Kivu – one of the African Great Lakes.
  • Akagera National Park – safaris with elephants, lions, and more.
